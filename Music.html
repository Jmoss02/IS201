Best Music

Caamp, Mumford and Sons, and the Lumineers

Caamp is a folk band from Ohio. They have an amazing nature feel to them that I love. They bring so much energy in every song whihc I love.

For my 15th birthday my family and some of my closest friends went to the Mumford and sons Concert. My dad loves them as well so it was great to be with him in one of the best concerts I have ever experienced.

In fall of 2023 I was able to go to the Lumineers and their new album is incredible. They offer meaningful lyrics that will keep you engaged the whole time.
